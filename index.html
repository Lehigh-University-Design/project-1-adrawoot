TCP/IP Protocols

TCP (Transmission Control Protocol) and IP (Internet Protocol) was designed by Vinton Cerf and Robert Kahn in 1973 in efforts to extend access to the ‘packet switching’ technology - Allowing for data to be sent to between two distant computers.
